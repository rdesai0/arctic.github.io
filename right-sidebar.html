Our Services

Flag Football

Trying to play a game of football just like they do in the NFL? Well, Arctic Sports has the best equipment for the job. We provide the flags, cones, spray paint, and a referee if requested.

Basketball

Want to shoot some hoops? Artic Sports offers basketball racks and jerseys to take your game to the next level, as well as referees with the purchase of the Bear package.

Soccer

Want to enjoy a game of soccer on a sunny day? We have you covered. We’ll provide goals, ball, and jerseys. All you have to worry about is bringing your friends and family!

Cricket

Cricket in America? Cricket is a popular game played readily in most countries. Arctic Sports provides bats, balls, wickets, padding (advanced equipment), and an umpire if requested.

Baseball

Arctic Sports is the premier outlet for baseball equipment. We provide bats, balls, gloves, spray paint, and an umpire if requested. Arctic Sports ensures everyone enjoys America's pasttime.

Ice/Field Hockey

To practice or just to try something new, we’re here to help! We provide field hockey sticks, nets, balls/pucks, padding, and cleats/skates. Just make sure you come ready to have fun!

Tennis

We’ve got everything you’ll ever need! We provide racquets, balls, and an umpire if purchased. Don’t stress about anything Arctic Sports has you covered!

Officiating Crew

Casual or pro, rules are a must! Purchase the Bear package for a referee! We put staff through training to ensure that they make your event better.

Field Equipment

Don't have a local public football field to play on? No problem. We provide spray paint, measurments, and lighting to make any local park parallel a real field.
