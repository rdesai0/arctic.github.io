Our Mission

Sustainable and affordable sporting goods for all sports fanatics

Arctic Sporting Goods is a startup rental business for teens, adults, and competitive athletes all-in-one. Our company was founded on the basis of ease and affordability. As high school students, we know the struggle of obtaining quality sporting equipment for recreational play. Arctic Sports is tailored for that community as well as more. Our business is designed to scale towards competitive league play so our target audience has the potential to expand. Our equipment is modeled after major sporting leagues and our referees and officials are certified through the National Association for Sports Officials and background checked. We are based in Miami, Florida and we are driven by the idea that sports is applicable to anyone anywhere.
